01 — About

Engineering at scale, leading with intent

I'm Prasanth Chintapalli (Prasanth Kumar Chintapalli), a Lead Software Engineer based in Bangalore, India, with 12+ years building cloud-native platforms in high-growth and enterprise environments. My work lives at the intersection of full stack development and people leadership — designing systems that scale to millions of requests while growing the engineers who own them.

I work end to end across the stack: Java and Spring Boot microservices on the backend, React and TypeScript with Micro Frontends on the frontend, all deployed on AWS. I've led platform re-architectures, driven cloud migrations, and shipped event-driven systems processing billions of events — with a constant focus on reliability, developer experience, and shipping value incrementally.

Leadership Philosophy

Set a clear technical vision, then create the conditions for autonomy. I lead through context, not control — coaching engineers to make great decisions when I'm not in the room.

Engineering Principles

  • Simple systems beat clever ones
  • Design for failure; measure everything
  • Optimize for change, not just today
  • Ship small, learn fast, iterate
